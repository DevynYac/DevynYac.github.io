I attend Kean University's Upward Bound Program, a wonderful program that aims to ready high school students for a college experience, especially during the summer. Here are some things we did during the summer session of 2023!

A PDF file of a written essay review on the second season of the Netflix TV Show, Sonic Prime.

During our English courses, we were constantly writing about things, my favorite thing I wrote here was a review to the Netflix TV Show's second season, Sonic Prime. This was entertaining to write for me as I am an avid fan of the Sonic the Hedgehog franchise, so I feel I know what I am talking about when it comes to this blue blur of a hedgehog. This review was, although written quick, it was also probably the most entertaining thing I have written during the summer session.
