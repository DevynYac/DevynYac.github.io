About



My name is Devyn Yac-Diaz and I am currently a 17 year old student that attends East Orange STEM Academy and wishes to pursue a career in computer science, hoping to study at New Jersey Institute of Technology.

My hobbies include doodling, game development, and playing games. I was first intrigued by game development at the beginning of my high school years when I found a YouTube video of an indie game developer making their own video game. Since then I've dabbled a bit and am genuinely interested in the application of computer science.

My top four choices for college to study computer science are NJIT, Montclair State University, Rutgers University, and Kean University. I plan to, during the summer of 2023, learn the Rust programming language and some of Java to prepare for my upcoming AP CSA class.
